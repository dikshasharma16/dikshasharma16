M.R. Citi Public School Balachaur

MR CITI PUBLIC SCHOOL BALACHAUR was established in 1999. It is located in Urban area. It is located in BALACHAUR block of NAWANSHAHR district of Punjab. The school consists of Grades from 1 to 12. English is the medium of instructions in this school. This school is approachable by all weather road. In this school academic session starts in April.
